WHY TO DONATE?

It Saves Lives

Ever wanted to be a superhero? You can be one just by donating blood which helps save 4 lives.

New blood cells

Once we donate blood, the body tries to restore the blood loss. This helps in the production of the new blood cells and maintain good health.

Psychological Upliftment

Beside all the heathy benefits that we obtain by donating blood, we also get the powerful benefit psychologically by helping the one in need.

How to Donate

Before Donation

  • Get plenty of sleep
  • Eat a healthy meal
  • At least 110 pounds
  • Feel PROUD!!!

Process

  • Registration
  • Mini-physical exam
  • Donation of blood
  • Refreshments!!!

After Donation

  • Drink extra fluids
  • Avoid strenuous physical activity
  • Rest if dizziness occurs
  • Motivate Others!!!
